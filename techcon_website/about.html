Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local meetup of software developers passionate about innovation. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most anticipated global tech conferences. Every year, thousands gather to share ideas, launch new products, and shape the future of technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect and empower the global tech community through inspiration, education, and collaboration. We believe in open access to knowledge and fostering innovation through inclusivity and opportunity.
